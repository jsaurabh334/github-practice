Frequently Asked Questions

How long are the courses?

Our course durations vary depending on the program. Bootcamps typically run for 12-16 weeks, while individual courses can range from 4-8 weeks.

Do I need prior experience?

No prior experience is necessary for beginner courses. However, advanced courses may require some background knowledge or experience in the subject.

Are the courses self-paced?

Some of our courses are self-paced, allowing you to learn at your own speed, while others follow a scheduled format with live instruction.

What kind of support is available?

We offer a range of support options including live Q&A sessions, discussion forums, and one-on-one mentoring with instructors.

Is there a job placement assistance?

Yes, we provide career support and job placement assistance to help you secure a position in the tech industry after course completion.
